On your new laptop, you must recreate it manually:

In VS Code, right-click the file list and choose "New File".

Name it exactly: .env.local

Paste your keys inside (you need to have these saved somewhere safe, or copy them from your Vercel/Supabase dashboard):

NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL=https://your-url.supabase.co
NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY=your-long-secret-key
